Get To Know Me

Hi there! I’m Marco Coralde, a passionate video editor and graphic designer based in Manila, Philippines. With over 2 years of experience, I’ve had the opportunity to work with businesses and brands, helping them bring their ideas to life through creative video editing and eye-catching graphic design.

I’m a quick learner, adaptable, and a team player who thrives in collaborative environments. I’m always on the lookout for new opportunities where I can grow, sharpen my skills, and contribute to meaningful projects. In my previous roles, I’ve successfully improved workflows, delivered projects on time, and consistently found ways to make processes more efficient. I take pride in being organized, detail-oriented, and someone who gets things done.
